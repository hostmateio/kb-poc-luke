The Conversion Process

Follow these steps for a successful conversion to Bp Premier, regardless of your source system.

1

Preparation

Backup your current system, verify system requirements, and ensure you have admin access to both systems.

2

Data Assessment

Review your current data, identify any potential issues, and clean up unnecessary records before conversion.

3

Conversion Setup

Download and install the appropriate conversion utility, and configure settings based on your needs.

4

Test Migration

Run a test conversion to identify any potential issues before proceeding with the full migration.

5

Verification

Check converted data for accuracy and completeness, ensuring all patient records, appointments, and clinical data transferred correctly.

6

Go-Live Planning

Schedule the final conversion during off-hours, prepare staff, and plan for any downtime.

7

Post-Conversion

Verify final data, train staff on any new workflows, and monitor system performance.
